Sammy the Turtle's Adventure

Courage and Patience

  • Sammy the turtle wanted to explore the big river. He walked slowly but carefully. On his way, he saw a fish trapped in a net.

  • Sammy helped the fish escape. The fish thanked Sammy and promised to help him later. Sammy continued his journey. He climbed rocks and crossed small streams.

  • Finally, he reached the other side safely. Sammy felt proud because he was patient and helped a friend along the way.
