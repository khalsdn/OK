The Brave Little Bird

Courage and Patience

  • Lina was a little bird who lived in a big forest. She was afraid to leave her nest. One morning, she saw other birds flying high in the sky. Lina wanted to join them but was scared.

  • Her mother said, 'You can do it!' Lina took a deep breath, spread her wings, and jumped. At first, she fell, but then she started to fly. She flew over trees and rivers and felt very happy. From that day, Lina was never afraid to try new things.
