## Page Content Breakdown

### 1. Cover Page (index.html)
- Beautiful illustration of children reading together
- Book title: "Better Communication Stories"
- Subtitle: "A Fun Way to Learn"
- Author: Mr Saidani Mnaouar
- "Start Reading" button leading to contents

### 2. Table of Contents (contents.html)
- Interactive story list with preview images
- 9 story cards with titles and themes
- Each card links to corresponding story page
- Progress tracking for completed stories

### 3. Story Pages (story01.html - story09.html)
- **Left Side**: Story text with large, readable font
- **Right Side**: Beautiful illustration matching the story
- **Bottom**: Vocabulary words and questions
- **Navigation**: Previous/Next buttons with page numbers
- **Progress**: Visual indicator showing current position

## Interactive Features
- Smooth page transitions with Anime.js
- Image galleries for each story using Splide.js
- Hover effects on navigation buttons
- Reading progress tracking
- Responsive design for tablets and mobile
