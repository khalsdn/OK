Emna Helps Her Grandma Jalila

Helping and Caring

  • Emna loved spending weekends at her grandma Jalila's house. One Saturday, her grandma Jalila felt tired. Emna noticed the dirty dishes in the sink and the messy living room. She decided to clean everything before her grandma Jalila woke up.

  • When Grandma Jalila saw the clean room, she smiled and hugged Emna. She said, 'You have such a kind heart, my dear.' Emna felt proud and happy to help someone she loved.
