# Better Communication Stories - Design Framework

## Design Philosophy
- **Target Audience**: Children aged 6-12 years
- **Visual Language**: Warm, friendly, and inviting with soft, approachable aesthetics
- **Emotional Impact**: Create a sense of wonder, learning, and joy in reading
- **Usability**: Large, clear text with intuitive navigation for young users

## Color Palette
- **Primary**: Soft coral (#FF8A80) - warm and welcoming
- **Secondary**: Gentle mint (#A5D6A7) - calming and fresh
- **Accent**: Warm yellow (#FFF176) - cheerful and energetic
- **Background**: Cream white (#FFFEF7) - soft on young eyes
- **Text**: Deep charcoal (#2E2E2E) - high contrast for readability

## Typography
- **Display Font**: "Fredoka One" - playful, rounded, child-friendly for headings
- **Body Font**: "Open Sans" - clean, readable for story text
- **Size Hierarchy**: Large headings (2.5rem), medium subheadings (1.5rem), comfortable body text (1.1rem)

## Visual Effects & Styling
- **Page Transitions**: Smooth slide animations between pages
- **Interactive Elements**: Gentle hover effects with soft shadows
- **Illustrations**: Warm, hand-drawn style with soft edges and bright colors
- **Layout**: Book-like proportions with generous white space
- **Navigation**: Large, colorful buttons with clear icons

## Animation Library Usage
- **Anime.js**: Page turn animations and text reveals
- **Splide.js**: Image carousels for story illustrations
- **Custom CSS**: Gentle floating animations for decorative elements

## Layout Structure
- **Cover Page**: Hero illustration with title and author
- **Table of Contents**: Interactive story selection
- **Story Pages**: Left text, right illustration layout
- **Navigation**: Always visible prev/next buttons with progress indicator
